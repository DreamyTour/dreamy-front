## The History of LGBT+ Pride Day

The Pride Day LGBT is celebrated every year on **June 28** in many parts of the world, although in reality, this entire month of June serves to celebrate Pride Month.

International LGBT (lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender) Pride Day is a highly significant date for members of the community. It stands as a reminder of the continuous struggle for equal rights, and is a vital call to prevent and forget the cases of violence, discrimination, and hate crimes of which members have historically been victims.
